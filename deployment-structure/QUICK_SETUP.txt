EASEARENA QUICK DEPLOYMENT GUIDE
================================

STEP 1: UPLOAD WEBSITE FILES
- Upload ALL files from public_html/ folder to your domain's public_html
- Your site structure should be:
  public_html/
  ├── index.html
  └── assets/
      ├── index-CKF7hNDU.css
      └── index-etYd2Bwa.js

STEP 2: IMPORT DATABASE
- Login to phpMyAdmin in cPanel
- Import: database/elijwoow_easearena_db.sql
- This creates your complete tournament database

STEP 3: CONFIGURE (Optional - for Node.js backend)
- Upload server/ files if your hosting supports Node.js
- Set DATABASE_URL in .env file
